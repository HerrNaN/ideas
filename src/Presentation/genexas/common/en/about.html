Exercise Assistant

The Exercise Assistant is an on-line tool to give feedback on stepwise solving of exercises in different domains.

The Exercise Assistant uses the functionality of the Strategy-based feedback, offered in the form of web services, from the IDEAS research group of the Utrecht University and the Open University, the Netherlands.
